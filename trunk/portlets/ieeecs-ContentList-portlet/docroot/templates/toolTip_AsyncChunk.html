• After the "initial" database query, the page will continue to pull records from the database, but asynchronously and in "chunks" in the background.
  • This "chunk" value will tell the page how many records to pull for each hit to the database.
  • When the "chunk" queries all add up to the "total" # of records configured, the asynchronous queries will stop.
  • Typical, safe values are between 20 and 500 records.
